Why Manor?

Living with others is great — managing the logistics isn't. Manor replaces the chaos of group chats, physical whiteboards, and scattered payment requests with a single, elegant source of truth. No more guessing who paid for what. No more nagging about chores. Just smooth, effortless cohabitation.
