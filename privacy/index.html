Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Manor ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Arbor AI Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Manor mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile information. You may also sign in using Google Sign-In, which provides us with your Google account email and basic profile information.

2.2 App Content

We collect and store content you create within the App, including:

  • Household information and member details
  • Financial transactions and expenses (Ledger)
  • Chore assignments, deadlines, and point values
  • Shopping list items
  • Messages and communications within your household

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We use Firebase services to collect:

  • Crash Data: Firebase Crashlytics collects crash reports and device state information to help us diagnose and fix issues.
  • Performance Data: App startup times, network request latency, and other performance metrics.
  • Installation ID: A unique anonymous identifier for push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's core functionality
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Enable collaboration between household members
  • Send push notifications for chore reminders, settlement updates, and household activity
  • Diagnose crashes and performance issues to improve app stability
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo (OTA Updates, Push Notifications) — Expo Privacy Policy

These third parties may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies. We do not control their data collection practices.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers managed by our backend infrastructure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and app content for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Crash and performance data may be retained in aggregate form for analytical purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
